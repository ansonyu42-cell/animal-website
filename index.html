  • Hammer head sharks are a type of shark that have a unique head shape that looks like a hammer.
  • They are found in warm waters around the world, including the Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Oceans.
  • Hammer head sharks are known for their excellent vision and ability to detect prey using electroreception.
  • They can grow up to 20 feet in length and weigh over 1,000 pounds.
  • Hammer head sharks are carnivorous and feed on fish, squid, and crustaceans.
